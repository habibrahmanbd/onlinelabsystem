Online lab system™ is a web application that establishes a network between the institutes and the students. Our lab teacher will enter the questions or tasks students have to done with the limited time. Student will get the tasks as well as code submitting box. Through this they can send the code to teacher as submission. This application will be launched because for a destination that is beneficial for both institutes and students. With this application, institute can take their lab in online (otherwise through their LAN connections). Students can give their lab and view their marks. This site is an attempt to remove the existing flaws in the manual system. The answers enter by the students are then evaluated and their score is calculated and displayed. Thus the lab can be managed by this application.
